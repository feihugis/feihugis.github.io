about

My name is Fei Hu. I’m a software engineer, and interested in the techniques involved in deep learning (e.g., TensorFlow), computer vision (e.g. OpenCV), big data (e.g. Spark, HDFS), and remote sensing, GIS (e.g. GDAL).
